About the Tech Expo

NEXORA25 brings future technology to the present—an immersive technical symposium for visionaries, innovators, and tech enthusiasts. Experience hands-on competitions, networking, and the spirit of engineering excellence backed by ISTE and Adhiyamaan College of Engineering [file:1].

Main Events & Competitions

  • PIXEL PITCH (Poster Making)
    • Team up to 3 members.
    • Topic: Cyber Security Awareness.
    • 1-page poster, present in 5 minutes.
    • Submission by Oct 10, 2025.
  • QUIZTEONICS (Tech Quiz)
    • Individual event.
    • 2 rounds, 45 questions.
    • Fast-paced rapid-fire finals.
  • QUANTUMFIX (Debugging)
    • Individual event.
    • Code or circuit debugging.
    • Complete within 30 minutes.
  • FUTURE FRAME (Paper Presentation)
    • Up to 3 members per team.
    • Abstract & PPT submission: Oct 6 & 10.
    • Presentation: 6 minutes, Q&A: 2 minutes.
